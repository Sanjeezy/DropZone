What is DropZone?

DropZone is a platform that allows ease of access for low income families or those currently homeless to find events near their location that are providing food or toiletries within the near future. The web platform provides an area for any organizations to post events they are hosting, and the events are then posted on a corresponding mobile app which will display the location, date, time, and directions to these events. We hope to provide fast and accurate about events happening locally that may be of assistance to those in need.
